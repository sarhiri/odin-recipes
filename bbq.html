Moroccan Sirloin Kebabs

Ingredients

Serves 3 to 4
  • 500 grams of sirloin steak cut into 2 inch cubes
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
  • 3 tablespoons very finely chopped cilantro
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
  • 2 red onions finely chopped
  • 1 small nub grated ginger
  • 1/2 freshly squeezed lemon (about 2 tablespoons)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl mix the meat with the rest of the ingredients.
  2. Using your hands, incorperate the seasonings throughout the meat so it absorbs the flavor.
  3. Let rest in the fridge for a minimum of 2 hours or overnight.
  4. When ready to serve, thread the meat through the skewers and grill to your liking.
