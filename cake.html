Lemon Olive Oil Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 lemon, grated for zest
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 3/4 cups extra virgin olive oil
  • 3/4 cups milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Farenheit
  2. Grease your baking disg.
  3. Blend the eggs in a blender for 2 minutes until light and foamy.
  4. Add sugar and blend for another 2 minutes or so until the liquid is thick and foamy.
  5. Add the vanilla and lemon zest and blend until foamy.
  6. While the blender is on, slowly add in the olive oil.
  7. Combine dry ingredients in a bowl.
  8. Add half the dry mixture, when that's all incorperated, add the other half.
  9. Add the milk, and blend until everything is well incorperated.
  10. Add to pan, bake for 35 minutes.
  11. If you want a crispy, crackly top, sprinkle sugar on top before baking.
